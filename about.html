Excellence in Sri Lankan Travel

Welcome to Golden Ceylon Holidays, your premier destination management company in Sri Lanka. We believe that traveling is more than just visiting places; it's about experiencing the soul of a destination, connecting with its people, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Founded on a deep love for our beautiful island, we specialize in offering uniquely tailored tours that reflect the incredible diversity of Sri Lanka. From the sun-kissed beaches of the south to the misty tea plantations of the hill country, our expert local guides ensure you see both the iconic sights and the hidden gems.

  • Over a decade of trusted experience
  • Highly trained, English-speaking local guides
  • Premium quality vehicle fleet for comfort
  • Exclusive luxury partner hotels & villas
